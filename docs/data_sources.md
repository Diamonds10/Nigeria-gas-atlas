Extracted via PDF text parsing (scripts/01_resource/08_process_opec_reserves.py) rather than a structured export — re-verify if OPEC changes PDF layout in future editions |

## Layer 4 — Demand
| Dataset | Source | URL | Date accessed | License | Coverage | Known limitations |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Cement plants | GEM wiki infoboxes, cross-verified against OSM Nominatim, plus public reporting — this study | https://www.gem.wiki ; https://nominatim.openstreetmap.org | 2026-07-21 | Derived/self-collected (underlying GEM data is CC-BY 4.0; OSM data is ODbL) | 10 plants: Dangote (Obajana, Gboko, Okpella, Ibese), Lafarge Africa (Ewekoro 1&2, Ashaka, Mfamosing), BUA (Kalambaina/Sokoto, Obu/Okpella) | No dedicated GEM tracker exists for cement (unlike extraction/pipelines/steel) — compiled plant-by-plant, then every coordinate cross-checked against an independently-mapped OSM facility polygon/node where one exists. BUA's Obu plant was initially conflated with Dangote's separate Okpella plant (~10 km apart) in an early draft; OSM's dedicated "BUA Cement Plc Okpella" node resolved this. Itori, Iluagba, Onigbedu, and Sagamu (in the demand-centers file below) remain unverified — no OSM match found |
| Fertiliser plants | GEM wiki infoboxes, cross-verified against OSM Nominatim, plus public reporting — this study | https://www.gem.wiki ; https://nominatim.openstreetmap.org | 2026-07-21 | Derived/self-collected (underlying GEM data is CC-BY 4.0; OSM data is ODbL) | 4 plants: Dangote Fertiliser (Lekki), Indorama Eleme (Port Harcourt), Notore Chemical (Onne, town-level precision only), Brass Fertilizer (Bayelsa, proposed) | Notore and Brass Fertilizer lack a plant-specific OSM match — coordinates are town/site-level (Onne town center; Brass Island, coincident with the Brass LNG Terminal site), not a verified facility footprint |
| Steel plants (GSPT) | Global Energy Monitor, via GreenInfo Network's public map viewer | https://greeninfo-network.github.io/steel-tracker/ | 2026-07-21 | CC-BY 4.0 (inferred, same caveat as other GreenInfo mirrors) | Current; 3 Nigerian plants: Delta Steel Co (Warri, operating), African Natural Resources & Mines (Kaduna, construction), Ajaokuta Steel (construction/long-stalled) | Ajaokuta has been under construction/non-operational for decades despite the "construction" status label — treat status as GEM's classification, not evidence of near-term completion. Both Ajaokuta and Delta Steel's coordinates were independently confirmed via OSM within 500m |
| Gas- and oil-fired power plants (GOGPT) | Global Energy Monitor, via GreenInfo Network's public map viewer — see Layer 2 entry | https://greeninfo-network.github.io/global-gas-plant-tracker/ | 2026-07-21 | CC-BY 4.0 (inferred, same caveat as other GreenInfo mirrors) | 193 unit-level records across ~60 Nigerian power stations | Physical dataset lives in `data/processed/02_infrastructure/gogpt_oil_gas_plants_nigeria.csv` — not duplicated here. This is total oil/gas power generation capacity, not specifically the share consumed as *gas* demand (some units run on liquid fuels) |
| Demand centers (cross-category master list) | User-supplied (thesis research), reconciled against GEM wiki and OSM Nominatim — this study | https://nominatim.openstreetmap.org | 2026-07-21 | Derived/self-collected (OSM data is ODbL) | 28 sites across fertiliser, refinery, cement, and steel — the most complete single Layer 4 list, including several plants (Notore, Brass Fertilizer, Sagamu, Itori, Iluagba, Onigbedu, African Foundries, Inner Galaxy Steel, Squire Steel, Jindal Steel [planned]) not found in any other source | `demand_centers_nigeria.csv` is the primary cross-cutting reference for Layer 4 site locations. An initial version of this table had two systematic errors, both now corrected using OSM-verified facility coordinates: (1) Dangote's Lekki refinery/fertiliser coordinate was ~53 km off — its longitude fell entirely outside the Lekki Free Trade Zone; (2) Okpella (Dangote) and Obu (BUA) cement plants had been given the same coordinate despite being distinct sites ~10 km apart. Eight further plants (Warri and Kaduna refineries; Ibese, Mfamosing, Gboko, Ashaka, Kalambaina cement; Ajaokuta and Premium/Delta steel) had smaller (5-33 km) errors, corrected the same way. 8 sites (Itori, Iluagba, Onigbedu, Sagamu cement; African Foundries, Inner Galaxy, Squire steel; Jindal Steel [planned]) have no OSM match and remain at their originally supplied precision — flagged per-row in the `coordinate_source` column |
